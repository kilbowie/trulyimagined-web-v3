# Truly Imagined — For Actors & Agencies

## What is Truly Imagined?

**Truly Imagined is the global registry and compliance infrastructure for human digital identity in AI.**

We provide actors with the tools to **register, control, and license** their digital identity for use in artificial intelligence systems.

---

## The Problem

AI can now recreate human voices, faces, and performances. This creates serious challenges:

- **Unauthorized usage** — Your likeness used without permission
- **No tracking** — No way to know when or how your identity is being used
- **Loss of control** — No ability to set boundaries or approve usage
- **No compensation** — Usage happening without licensing or payment
- **Legal uncertainty** — No clear compliance framework

---

## Our Solution

Truly Imagined provides **three core layers of infrastructure**:

### 1. Identity Registry
Register your digital identity in a global, verified registry. This establishes your ownership and creates a foundation of trust.

### 2. Consent Ledger
Every permission decision is recorded in an immutable, timestamped ledger. This creates a complete audit trail of what you've approved and what you haven't.

### 3. Licensing Control
You define the rules for how your identity can be used — what projects, what types of content, what compensation. You own the decision.

## How It Works

### For Actors:

1. **Register** your identity in the Truly Imagined registry
2. **Define** your consent boundaries and licensing preferences  
3. **Receive** licensing requests from creators and studios
4. **Approve or deny** based on your preferences
5. **Track** all usage through your dashboard
6. **Get compensated** according to your licensing terms

### For Agencies:

1. **Onboard** your clients to the registry (we can help)
2. **Manage** their consent and licensing preferences
3. **Review** usage and licensing requests on their behalf
4. **Audit** all activity through immutable logs
5. **Demonstrate** compliance to studios and platforms

## Questions?

### "How is this different from my existing contracts?"
This is infrastructure that **enforces** your contracts. It provides the registry, audit trail, and compliance layer that makes your rights enforceable in AI systems.

### "What about my agency — do they still manage my deals?"
Yes. Agencies can manage their clients' preferences and review licensing requests. This is a tool that **empowers agencies** to protect their clients better.

### "Is my data secure?"
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security (Auth0, AWS, encrypted databases) and never share your data without your explicit consent.

### "What if I don't want to be used in AI at all?"
That's your choice. Registering creates a record of **non-consent** which is just as important as consent. It establishes your position clearly.

### "How do you make money?"
We charge a small platform fee on licensing transactions. Actors keep the majority of licensing revenue — you own your identity, you own the economics.
